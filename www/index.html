This web app is used to find out what's near a particular Airbnb. Just paste the link of an Airbnb you're interested in and enter a search query such as "gyms," "laundry", or "supermarkets", wait until the coordinates show up on the screen, then hit Search. The blue bubble shows a 1 km radius of the approximated Airbnb location, and results are shown up to 2km away. You can also search for more specific stuff like a street name or certain attraction.

Nearbnb is an open-source project by Paul Bischoff built using R Shiny and the Google Maps Javascript API. Check out the Github repo here.

I don't collect any of your data, but all users are subject to the Google Maps terms of use.
